Creating a Sequential Multiplier

Winter 2025

Objective

    Throughout my labs in my Digital Systems I course (CIS*3120), we slowly created integrated circuits (ICs) in LogicWorks that would layer together to make a sequential multiplier. Here I'll go through the crucial ICs I created. I aim to add little about the course itself to respect the intellectual property of the professor.

Plan

    I'm using the youtber Player Conveyor's youtube video "I Built the World's Comfiest Mouse (ft. Bambu Lab A1)" as inspiration for my steps. I've started off by making a mock up mouse out of clay and getting a 3d model from a lidar sensor. The next step is to send it into blender and solidworks to clean up the design.     After I am happy with the outer shape, I'll gut a cheap mouse to use it's circuit board for the first iteration of my mouse. Then, I will try to create a custom PCB for the mouse.
