No Money - Film Case Study

January 2020

Objective

    This short was created for a class assignment that asked us to do a case study on a director. I chose Tarantino and, more specifically, studied his hand-held camera motion and the colours used. In the last scene of "Inglorious Bastards." I tried to emulate the dichotomy of nonchalant and stressed characters as in Tarantino's film.

Reflection

    This was made a few years ago, so looking back, I can see that my videography skills were a bit rough, and my audio editing wasn't amazing. However, I still appreciate the process I went through. I hope I get to work on more projects like this in the future.
