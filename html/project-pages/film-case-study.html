No Money - Film Case Study

January 2020

Objective

    This short was created for a class assignment that asked us to do a case study on a director. I chose Tarintino and, more specifically, studied his hand-held camera motion and colours used in the last scene of "Inglorious Bastards." I tried to emulate the dichotomy of nonchalant characters and stressed characters, having the non-English speakers stressed like in Tarintino's film.

Film

Reflection

    This was made a few years ago now, so looking at it I can see that my videography skills were a bit rough and my audio editing was not amazing. However, I still appreciate the process I went through. Planning and researching for the film forced me to look at some of my favourite movies differently and I got to film with my family and friends. I hope I get to work on more projects like this in the future.
